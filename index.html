ABOUT US

Who We Are

Our company operates as a general trading establishment located in Sharjah–UAE. Our main activity includes metal trading and waste collections involving woods, PP, LDPE, HDPE, LLD & HMS, HABS, ABS, MS punch, SS boring & punch, Aluminium, Copper, Brass, Magnet, Lead dross, and PCB boards, along with various other purchased goods. We also handle projects involving harmful and non-harmful material (such as batteries and high-radiation devices) as part of our import and export operations.

We provide skilled labour, transport, and safety equipment in compliance with UAE regulations. Our strong work ethic and reliability have helped us maintain long-term partnerships with our clients. We continue working with commitment and dedication to ensure complete satisfaction for all our customers.

Our Vision

The proprietor of the company aims to establish our business hub as the leading buyer of waste plastics and iron scrap in the Gulf region within the next three years. Continued expansion of our scrap processing capacity will support future growth in recyclable material trading.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of customer service and product quality assurance. Over the past 2 to 3 years, we have experienced consistent growth through increased post-consumer scrap purchases from our regular suppliers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver dependable, responsible, and environmentally conscious recycling services that support long-term sustainability across the region. We aim to strengthen the recycling ecosystem by ensuring safe handling, processing, and trading of metals and other recyclable materials.

Through continuous improvement, operational transparency, and strong supplier relationships, we strive to offer consistent quality, prompt service, and reliable trading solutions to all our clients.
